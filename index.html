How to swap?

Easy as Browse, Borrow, and Return

Step One: Browse Your Area , Find Your Item

Search for items available in your local community. Use filters to find clothing based on size, type, or occasion. Once you find something you like, check the item's details, including the condition, owner's notes, and whether it's available for rent or swap.

Step Two: Send a Swap Offer

Reach out to the owner through the platform to request the item or propose a swap with something you own.

Step Four: Enjoy Your Outfit!

Use the item for your event or daily wear while adhering to the owner's care instructions.

Step Five: Follow Return Instructions

Check the owner's description to see if cleaning is required before returning the item.

Step Six: Use the Preset Drop Off Location

Return the item to the agreed-upon location set during the initial messaging exchange.
