What is Swathe?

Swathe empowers students to redefine their relationship with fashion. By renting or swapping clothing within your community, you can express your style, reduce textile waste, and save money—all while supporting sustainable practices [1].

Why swap?

  • Keep clothes out of landfill [1]
  • Swap Local [1]
  • Extend clothing life [1]

How to swap?

Easy as Browse, Borrow, and Return [1]

Step One: Browse Your Area & Find Your Item [2]

Search for items available in your local community. Use filters to find clothing based on size, type, or occasion. Once you find something you like, check the item's details, including the condition, owner's notes, and whether it’s available for rent or swap [2].

Step Two: Send a Swap Offer [2]

Reach out to the owner through the platform to request the item or propose a swap with something you own [2].

Step Four: Enjoy Your Outfit! [2]

Use the item for your event or daily wear while adhering to the owner’s care instructions [2].

Step Five: Follow Return Instructions [3]

Check the owner’s description to see if cleaning is required before returning the item [3].

Step Six: Use the Preset Drop Off Location [3]

Return the item to the agreed-upon location set during the initial messaging exchange [3].

Sustainability Impact [3]

Swathe helps reduce textile waste and supports sustainable practices [1].
